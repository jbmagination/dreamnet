DESCRIPTION
GLASKLART SUPPORTS IOS7, IOS8 and iOS9 (and unofficially iOS10 to iOS13).
Glasklart works with Anemone and SnowBoard.
Glasklart is an out-of-the-way, transparent theme that lets you really showcase your wallpaper.
Glasklart is swedish, it means "clear as glass".
Now includes 9.500+ high resolution icons which have been graciously crafted by the community.
Need to an icon for your favorite app?
Check out Glasklart on Github and help make the theme even more amazing!
The wallpaper used in the screenshots, 'Candybars', can be downloaded from my website: http://www.maxrudberg.com/
